Warning Signs Your Wiring Is Failing

  • Lights flicker or dim when appliances turn on
  • Outlets feel warm or hot to the touch
  • Burning or fishy smell near outlets or walls
  • Breakers that trip frequently or won't stay on
  • Two-prong (ungrounded) outlets throughout the home
  • Home was built before 1985 with original wiring
