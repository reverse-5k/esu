6 Emergencies We Fix Instantly

If you notice any of these warning signs, cut power to the area and call us immediately. Waiting can turn a repair into a disaster.

Dead Outlets & Switches

Outlets that don't work or feel loose when you plug something in are a major shock hazard. We replace them with safe, modern receptacles.

Flickering Lights

Flickering isn't a ghost — it's a loose connection. Loose connections cause arcing, which is the leading cause of electrical fires in homes.

Burning Smell From Panel

A burning or plastic smell near your panel is a five-alarm warning. Cut the main breaker and call us. This is a fire waiting to happen.

Partial Power Loss

Lost power in just half your house? We have the advanced diagnostic tools to pinpoint the break in your wiring without tearing down walls unnecessarily.

Breaker Won't Reset

A breaker that trips immediately after resetting is protecting you from a short circuit or overload. Forcing it can melt wires inside your walls.

Sparks or Buzzing

Any visible spark or audible buzz from an outlet or panel requires immediate attention. These are live fault conditions that escalate fast.
