About ESU

We're a South Florida electrical company. Not a franchise, not a call center. Just a local crew that's been wiring Miami homes and businesses since 2016.

How ESU Got Started

ESU was founded in 2016 by Ivan Alonso, a South Florida native who learned his trade in the military. After completing his electrical apprenticeship in the armed forces right out of high school, Ivan spent over 15 years honing his craft before passing his state licensing exams in both Florida and North Carolina.

He started ESU with one truck and a straightforward idea: do honest electrical work, treat every customer's home like your own, and never cut corners. Word spread. The company grew.

"What started as a one-man-and-a-truck operation has grown into a full-service electrical contracting company with 10 full-time employees and 5 trucks on the road." — Ivan Alonso, Owner & State Certified Electrical Contractor

Today ESU serves residential and commercial clients across South Florida — from single-family homes and custom builds to office fit-outs, retail spaces, religious facilities, and educational buildings.

What We Commit to, Every Single Job

Inviting a technician into your home requires trust. We earn it — before, during, and after every job. Every person we send to your door is background-checked, drug-tested, uniformed, and licensed. When they knock, you know exactly who they are and why they're there.

Our Code of Ethics

  • Honest pricing. We quote before we touch anything. The price you hear is the price you pay. No surprise charges.
  • Respect for your home. Shoe covers on, drop cloths down, mess cleaned up before we leave. Always.
  • No shortcuts. Every job is done to code. The wrong way puts your family at risk, and that's not something we're willing to do.

Our 100% Satisfaction Guarantee

If you're not completely happy with our work, let us know. We'll come back and make it right. No debate, no runaround. That's been our standard since day one.
