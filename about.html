About ESU

We're a South Florida electrical company. Not a franchise, not a call center. Just a local crew that's been wiring Miami homes and businesses since 2016.

How ESU Got Started

ESU was founded in 2016 by Ivan Alonso, a South Florida electrician who served in the United States Marine Corps before building a career in the electrical trade. He spent years honing his craft before passing his state licensing exams and going out on his own.

He started ESU with one truck and a straightforward idea: do honest electrical work, treat every customer's home like your own, and never cut corners. Word spread. The company grew.

"What started as a one-man-and-a-truck operation has grown into a full-service electrical contracting company with 10 full-time employees and 5 trucks on the road." — Ivan Alonso, Owner & State Certified Electrical Contractor

Today ESU serves residential and commercial clients across South Florida — from single-family homes and custom builds to office fit-outs, retail spaces, religious facilities, and educational buildings.

What We Commit to, Every Single Job

Inviting a technician onto your property requires trust. We earn it — before, during, and after every job. Every person we send is background-checked, drug-tested, uniformed, and licensed. When they arrive, you know exactly who they are and why they're there.

Our Code of Ethics

  • Honest pricing. We quote before we touch anything. The price you hear is the price you pay. No surprise charges.
  • Respect for your space. Shoe covers on, drop cloths down, mess cleaned up before we leave. Every job, every time.
  • No shortcuts. Every job is done to code. The wrong way puts people at risk, and that's not something we're willing to do.

Our 100% Satisfaction Guarantee
