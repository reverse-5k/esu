Why Settle For A Portable Generator?

Portable generators are loud, dangerous, require constant refueling in the rain, and only power a few extension cords. A Whole-Home Standby Generator is the ultimate upgrade.

Hands-Free Operation

The system senses a power outage and automatically turns itself on within 10 seconds. You don't have to lift a finger or go outside in a storm.

Whole-Home Comfort

Unlike portables, our installed systems can power your entire electrical panel, meaning you can take hot showers and run your central AC without worry.

Boosts Home Value

A permanently installed standby generator is a massive selling point in Florida, often recuperating over 60% of its cost in increased property value.
