Commercial Lighting Services in Miami

We handle every type of commercial lighting installation and retrofit — from a single-suite office to a full warehouse campus or parking structure.

Office LED Upgrades

Replace outdated fluorescent troffers with modern LED panels. Better color rendering, less flicker, and dramatically lower energy costs. We phase the work so your team stays productive throughout.

Warehouse High-Bay LED

LED high-bay fixtures deliver 2–3x more usable light than metal halide or T5 fluorescent systems, with no warm-up time and zero lamp replacements for years. Dramatically improves worker visibility and safety.

Retail & Showroom Lighting

LED track lighting, recessed fixtures, and display lighting that make merchandise look better and reduce energy costs at the same time. We coordinate around your store hours and merchandising layouts.

Parking Lot & Exterior

Replace sodium vapor and metal halide parking lot fixtures with LED shoebox lights. Better coverage, dramatically lower maintenance costs, and significant energy savings on one of the highest-draw circuits on most commercial properties.

Emergency & Exit Lighting

Code-required emergency lighting and exit signs that actually work when grid power fails. We install, test, and provide all documentation required for certificate of occupancy and annual fire inspections.

Pre-Project Energy Audit

Before we quote anything, we assess your current fixtures, calculate projected wattage reduction, and give you a realistic monthly savings estimate in writing. No guesswork — you'll know the numbers before you commit to a single fixture.

We Work Around Your Business Hours

The most common reason businesses delay LED upgrades is the fear of disruption. In reality, most commercial lighting retrofits can be phased or scheduled around your operation:

  • Offices: Floor-by-floor phasing or weekend installation keeps employees working throughout the project.
  • Retail & Restaurants: We schedule work during off-hours or overnight so your business never closes for lighting.
  • Warehouses: Section-by-section work keeps your operation running during normal business hours.
  • Parking Lots: Night work with temporary lighting keeps the lot safe while fixtures are replaced.
