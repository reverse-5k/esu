Commercial Lighting Services

We handle every type of commercial lighting installation and retrofit, from a single-suite office to a full warehouse or parking structure.

Office LED Upgrades

Replace outdated fluorescent troffers with modern LED panels. Better color rendering, less flicker, and dramatically lower energy costs — with minimal disruption during business hours.

Warehouse High-Bay LED

High-bay LED fixtures deliver 2–3x more usable light than outdated metal halide or fluorescent T5 systems, with no warm-up time and zero maintenance for years.

Retail & Showroom Lighting

LED track lighting, recessed fixtures, and display lighting that make merchandise look better and cut your energy bill at the same time. We work around your store hours.

Parking Lot & Exterior

Replace sodium vapor and metal halide parking lot fixtures with LED shoebox lights. Better coverage, lower maintenance, and significant energy savings on one of the highest-draw circuits in most commercial buildings.

Emergency & Exit Lighting

Code-required emergency lighting and exit signs that actually work when power goes out. We install, test, and provide the documentation required for your certificate of occupancy.

Energy Audit & Rebate Help

We assess your current fixtures, calculate projected savings, and prepare the documentation you need to apply for FPL commercial energy efficiency rebates — which can offset 30–50% of project costs.
