A 120V Outlet Is Not a Charger

Plugging your EV into a standard household outlet adds roughly 3–5 miles of range per hour. You'd need 30+ hours to fill a dead battery. A dedicated Level 2 circuit changes everything.

Level 2 — 240V Circuit

We run a dedicated 240V, 50-amp circuit from your electrical panel to the garage or parking area — the same kind of circuit your dryer or electric range uses. This is the right way to charge a vehicle at home.

Works With Any EV

Whether you drive a Tesla, Ford F-150 Lightning, Chevy Bolt, Rivian, BMW, or any other electric or plug-in hybrid, we install the right outlet or hardwired unit for your specific vehicle and setup.

Smart Charger Ready

Want a WiFi-enabled smart charger that lets you schedule off-peak charging to save on your electric bill? We can hardwire any brand — ChargePoint, Wallbox, JuiceBox, or the Tesla Wall Connector.
