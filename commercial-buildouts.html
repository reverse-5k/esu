Commercial Electrical Buildout Services

From the first conduit stub-out to the final permit sign-off, we handle the entire electrical scope of your tenant improvement or new construction project.

Office Buildouts

Power and data rough-in, lighting circuits, break room and server room dedicated circuits, and panel work for new or renovated office suites — from a single-tenant space to multi-floor corporate fit-outs.

Restaurant Electrical

High-draw kitchen circuits for commercial fryers, ovens, and refrigeration units. Hood ventilation wiring, walk-in cooler power, hand sink heaters, and health department compliance from day one.

Retail Space Wiring

Track lighting, display circuits, POS power runs, fitting room outlets, and storefront signage power. We coordinate around your merchandise layouts and fixture plans before the first nail goes in.

Medical & Dental Offices

Dedicated circuits for imaging equipment, sterilization units, exam room power, and generator transfer switching. We understand NFPA 99 health care facility requirements and code the work accordingly.

Warehouse & Light Industrial

High-bay lighting rough-in, 3-phase power distribution, dock door operators, equipment circuits, and service entrance upgrades for high-load operations throughout South Florida.

Code Violations & Corrections

From Bid to Certificate of Occupancy

Here's how a typical commercial buildout moves from our first conversation to the final city inspection.

1

Scope & Estimate

We review electrical drawings (or assist in creating them), provide a written itemized estimate, and confirm the schedule before any contract is signed. No vague line items.

2

3

Device installation, panel termination, fixture hang, and final city inspection. We don't leave the jobsite until every item on the punch list is cleared.
