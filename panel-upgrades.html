Is Your Electrical Panel A Ticking Time Bomb?

Your electrical panel is the heart of your home's power system. If it's more than 20 years old, it was never designed to handle today's smart TVs, HVAC systems, and EV chargers. Look out for these critical warning signs:

Constantly Tripping Breakers

If running the microwave and the hair dryer at the same time trips a breaker, your panel is dangerously undersized for your lifestyle.

Burning Smells or Heat

If your breaker box feels warm to the touch or smells like burning plastic, call us immediately. This is an urgent fire hazard.

FPE or Zinsco Panels

If your home was built before 1990 and has a Federal Pacific (FPE) or Zinsco panel, it is functionally obsolete and known to cause fires. It must be replaced.
