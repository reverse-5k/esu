Three Layers of Lighting Design

Great lighting isn't just bright. It's layered. Our installers plan all three tiers so your space looks intentional, not just illuminated.

Ambient — Base Layer

The foundation of any room. Recessed LED downlights (can lights / high-hats) provide even, comfortable fill light throughout the space without shadows or glare.

Task — Focused Layer

Directed light for work surfaces — under-cabinet strips in the kitchen, vanity lighting in bathrooms, and reading lights in offices or bedrooms.

Accent — Detail Layer

The finishing touch. Wall sconces, landscape up-lights, and cove lighting that frame architectural features and add depth and drama to a room.
